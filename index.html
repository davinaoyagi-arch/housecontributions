INDUSTRY GROUPS

Where cycle dollars cluster

Rule-based

Industry method: names and reported employers are matched to a reviewable keyword taxonomy. Matson, Turo, and Hawaiian Airlines are classified as Transportation & Logistics. Ambiguous records remain Other / unclassified. Contributor aliases remain separate unless basic case, spacing, and punctuation normalization resolves them. The 2018–2020 cycle is clipped to January 1, 2020.
